About The Show

Al-Naddam is a Syrian drama series shown in Ramadan 2016, which revolves around the story of a forty-year-old TV writer named Orwa, who begins his story on the night of the fall of the Baghdad statue, through which he reviews historical events and political junctures from 2003 until now.

Scenes in the series range from colorful from side and white, black and cold blue, as the rich colors express the past and what is contained in the memory of the hero "Orwa", while the white, black and cold blue express the sad diaries and observations of the hero that describe what Syria has come to and about its bleak present and rejected by the Syrian people, The story of the series Al-Naddam is told stories about a text by writer and screenwriter Hassan Sami Yusef.
